About

The Science Behind the Scientist

I am a PhD scholar in Neurogenetics at the Department of Human Genetics and Molecular Biology, Bharathiar University, Coimbatore. My doctoral research focuses on uncovering the genetic and molecular mechanisms that underpin neurological conditions, with a particular interest in oxidative stress pathways and gene mutation analysis.

My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Science in Biotechnology from PSG College of Arts and Science, followed by a Master of Science in Human Genetics and Molecular Biology at Bharathiar University. During my Master's, I conducted award-winning research on the neuroprotective effects of Convolvulus pluricaulis and PARK7 gene mutation in SH-SY5Y neuroblastoma cell lines.

I have co-authored peer-reviewed research in the International Review of Neurobiology and Apoptosis, and contributed a book chapter to Advances in Protein Chemistry and Structural Biology.
